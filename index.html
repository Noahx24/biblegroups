Built for Methodist churches.

ChurchFlow brings your class groups, volunteer schedules, weekly verses, and family programmes into one calm, beautifully designed app. It's being built specifically for Methodist churches - leaders, volunteers, parents, and members all in one place.

Privacy and safety first. Children's information is handled with explicit parental consent, an audit log, and full subject-access rights - compliant with South Africa's POPIA from day one.

What you'll be able to do.

Everything members, volunteers, and leaders need - in one app.

Schedule & roster

Class meetings, volunteer slots, and birthdays on one shared calendar. One tap to accept or decline.

Verse of the week

Leaders share a Bible verse for the week in KJV, ESV, NIV, or NLT, with a daily reading plan that travels with you.

Groups & leaders

Find your class, see who's leading, and join the volunteer teams you serve on - in person, online, or both.

Family & children's programmes

Register children for youth, Sunday school, and holiday clubs. Allergies and emergency contacts kept safe under POPIA.

Church news & newsletters

Every newsletter, archived and searchable. Catch up on what you missed in seconds.

Hymn book in your language

Methodist hymns in Xhosa, Sotho, and English with full search - sing along on Sunday or quietly through the week.
